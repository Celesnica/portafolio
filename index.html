BIOGRAPHY


Celesnica, with about 3 years of experience, is a 17 y/o music producer currently live in Hong Kong. Experimenting with different elements in different music genres, he has combined different styles of music together. His genre includes future bass, melodic dubstep, drum and bass, glitch hop, color bass, future garage, and so on. He has received support from some sensational artist around the world (mainly in mainland China), including Vesk Green, PRIYANX, F-777, Willim, Dawave, Roger.J, and N2V. For the three years before 2026, he was engaged in behind-the-scenes music production and participated in activities as an underground artist. At the beginning of 2026, he decided to produce and release songs as an independent musician. As a member of Guangzhou Zhacai Music Studio and the leader of TRA, he also helps propagate electronic music culture in China.

PROJECTS

GLITCHWAVE


A project of combining traditional and classic Synthwave with modern glitch music genres like Glitch Hop and Complextro. While blending the emotional aesthetics of synthwave and the futuristic sound designs in modern bass music, Celesnica also explore new styles of arrangements and mixing.

远日点


This is a narrative-driven prject that try to use a narrative structure of novel in composing music. The main theme of the project is about the emergence, evolution, interaction of civilisations in the Universe
